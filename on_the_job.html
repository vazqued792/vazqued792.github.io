On the job

 JOBSplus! offers a great incentive to employers who would like to take a chance on hiring someone who may  have limited skills but who has great potential. With On-the-Job Training, we assist recipients of  Temporary Assistance with transitioning off the welfare system while you reduce your training costs..

 Through On-the-Job Training, Onondaga County pays up to 50% of a participant's gross wages for up to six months . We find this arrangement offers an easy transition to the work place with limited risk for the employer. To learn more about this program, please contact a JOBSplus! job developer at (315)-442-3242 and ask to speak to our Job Developer about the OJT programs, or by email by clicking the link below.
